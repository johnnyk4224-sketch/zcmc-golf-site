Committee Responsibilities

The Sunset Golf League is a committee of the ZCMC and is subject to its bylaws and rules. The ZCGC Board of Directors appoints a League Administrator to run the league and perform scorekeeping duties.

Back to top ↑
Purpose

The purpose of the Sunset League is to promote competition between members of the ZCGC.

Back to top ↑
Membership
  1. Membership in the Zenith City Golf Club.
  2. Current team dues paid.
  3. Current individual dues paid.
Back to top ↑
League Rules
  1. Teams are encouraged to have five members. Minimum four, maximum ten.
  2. Players must play at least two matches to qualify for playoffs.
  3. Captains are responsible for roster eligibility and tee times.
  4. USGA rules govern play except where modified by the league or Enger Park.
Back to top ↑
Scoring
  1. 1 point per hole won by low net score.
  2. Ties split 1/2 point.
  3. 10 points per foursome. 20 points per match.
Back to top ↑
Playoffs
  1. Top eight teams from each night qualify.
  2. Seeded by winning percentage.
  3. Tiebreakers include head-to-head, highest single-night points, most nightly wins, then coin flip.
Back to top ↑
Verifying Scores of Matches
  1. Verify correct handicaps.
  2. Verify score totals.
  3. Sign and turn in scorecards.
Back to top ↑
Handicap System

League uses GHIN scoring.

Back to top ↑
To Help Speed Up Play
  1. Be ready for your tee time.
  2. Maximum score counted is double bogey for handicap purposes.
  3. Keep up with the group ahead of you.
